The Technology

What is an Educational Inflatable Dome?

It is a premium, highly portable planetarium-style environment configured directly within your school premises. Students step inside a dynamic 360° theater system where tailored cinematic content projects completely around them.

By wrapping educational tracks across a complete hemispherical array, complex subjects convert directly into memorable visual events that enhance deep memory retention.

360° Projection

High-definition wraparound display system covering your full vision scale.

Surround Audio

Deep atmospheric audio fields ensuring total spatial clarity.
